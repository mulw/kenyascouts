Who We Are

Guardians of the Scout Tradition

Vigogo is the prestigious Elders' Council of the Kenya Scouts Association — a brotherhood and sisterhood of seasoned Scout leaders who have dedicated decades to shaping Kenya's youth. We serve as the custodians of Scouting values across all 47 counties.

Our mission is to preserve Scouting heritage, mentor the next generation of leaders, and maintain the highest standards of integrity, service, and excellence in Kenya.
