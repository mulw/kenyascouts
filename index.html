Who We Are

Custodians of a Century of Scouting

Vigogo is the Elders' Council of the Vigogo Scouts of Kenya — a fellowship of experienced Scout leaders who have given decades to building Kenya's youth. A Kigogo (singular) is a revered elder whose wisdom, service record, and commitment to Scouting earns them a place in this distinguished council.

Our Vigogos mentor active leaders, advise county and national Scout councils, and preserve the traditions that have made Kenyan Scouting a model for East Africa for over a century.

Heritage
Preserving Scouting traditions and institutional knowledge across generations.
Mentorship
Guiding the next tier of Scout leaders with lived experience and wisdom.
Service
Continued commitment to community and national development across Kenya.
Unity
Connecting Vigogos from all 47 counties in shared purpose and brotherhood.
