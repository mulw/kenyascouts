Who We Are

Custodians of a Century of Scouting

Vigogo is the prestigious Elders' Council of the Kenya Scouts Association — a brotherhood and sisterhood of experienced Scout leaders who have given decades to building Kenya's youth. We serve as the living memory and moral compass of the Scouting movement nationwide.

Our elders mentor active leaders, advise county and national councils, and preserve the traditions that have made Kenyan Scouting a model for East Africa for over a century.

Heritage
Preserving Scouting traditions and institutional knowledge across generations.
Mentorship
Guiding the next tier of Scout leaders with lived experience and wisdom.
Service
Continued commitment to community and national development.
Unity
Connecting elders from all 47 counties in shared purpose.
